<p>Psychphinder is an app that will let you search quotes from all the episodes and movies of the TV show Psych.</p>
<p>&#127821; <b>Features </b> &#127821;</p>
<p><ul><li>Search quotes from all the episodes and movies of Psych.</li>
   <li>Save your favorite quotes.</li>
   <li>Backup and restore your saved quotes.</li>
   <li>View the references made in each episode.</li>
   <li>Search what a reference means in your prefered search engine.</li>
   <li>Share/save a quote as image or text.</li>
   <li>Create wallpapers of your favorite quotes from the show.</li>
   <li>Choose from the three themes available.</li></ul>
<p>You can also share your backup file with others if you want, even between different platforms!</p>
